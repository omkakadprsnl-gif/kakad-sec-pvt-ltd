About Kakad Security

Maharashtra's trusted security partner with over a decade of excellence

Your Security, Our Priority

Founded in Jalna, Kakad Security PVT LTD has grown to become one of Maharashtra's most respected security service providers. With our headquarters in Jalna, we serve clients across the entire state, from Mumbai to Nagpur, Pune to Aurangabad.

Our team of over 500 rigorously trained security professionals undergoes continuous training in modern security protocols, emergency response, and customer service. Each guard is background verified and equipped with the knowledge to handle diverse security scenarios.
